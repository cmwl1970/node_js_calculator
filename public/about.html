Welcome to the Running Total Calculator!

This calculator is designed to provide an easy and efficient way to perform everyday calculations.

Features:

  • Basic arithmetic operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Positive and Negative toggle button, and Percentage.
  • Running total display for continuous calculations.
  • Light and dark theme options for comfortable use in any lighting.
  • History feature to keep track of your past calculations.

How to Use:

Simply enter your numbers and select the desired operation. The running total will update automatically.

Switch between light and dark themes using the theme toggle at the top right corner of the page.

View your calculation history on the right side of the calculator.
